How it works

90-minute session
Rules (simple)
  • The session is divided into Days (Rounds) of about 2–3 minutes.
  • Each day may have 0–3 news releases (usually 1–2).
  • When news hits, the impacted stocks become highly volatile and trend strongly until that day closes.
  • Trades execute using bid/ask + slippage + fee, similar to real markets.
  • Your goal: finish with the highest total equity (cash + holdings value).
Quick tip
Focus on the stocks marked as DIRECT/SECTOR impact after a headline — those react the most.
